# 1. Your Most Repeated Grammar Mistakes

## Mistake 1: Although + but

### Wrong
Although online learning is useful but it may reduce communication.

### Correct
Although online learning is useful, it may reduce communication.

### Rule
Use **although** OR **but**, not both together.

### Memorize
Although technology is useful, it can distract students.  
Although online learning is convenient, it may reduce face-to-face communication.  
Although public transport is affordable, many people still prefer private cars.

---

## Mistake 2: helps + object + base verb

### Wrong
Reading helps students improving vocabulary.  
Online learning helps students learning from home.

### Correct
Reading helps students improve vocabulary.  
Online learning helps students learn from home.

### Rule
**helps + person + base verb**

### Memorize
Reading helps students improve vocabulary.  
Technology helps people communicate easily.  
Education helps students get better jobs.  
Online learning helps students study from home.

---

## Mistake 3: opportunity to + verb

### Wrong
Online learning gives students the opportunity study from home.

### Correct
Online learning gives students the opportunity to study from home.

### Rule
**opportunity to + base verb**

### Memorize
Online learning gives students the opportunity to study from home.  
Education gives students the opportunity to get better jobs.  
Digital platforms give students the opportunity to learn from qualified teachers.

---

## Mistake 4: affect vs effect

### Wrong
Technology may negatively effect students concentration.

### Correct
Technology may negatively affect students' concentration.

### Rule
**Affect** = verb  
**Effect** = noun

### Memorize
Technology may negatively affect students' concentration.  
Social media can affect students' academic performance.  
Technology can have a negative effect on concentration.

---

## Mistake 5: students' concentration

### Wrong
students concentration

### Correct
students' concentration

### Rule
When something belongs to plural students, use **students'**.

### Memorize
Technology may reduce students' concentration.  
Social media can affect students' academic performance.  
Reading improves students' vocabulary.

---

## Mistake 6: a/an + singular noun

### Wrong
a important role  
a employment opportunity  
a better opportunities

### Correct
an important role  
an employment opportunity  
better opportunities

### Rule
Use **an** before vowel sounds.  
Do not use **a/an** before plural nouns.

### Memorize
Education plays an important role in society.  
Getting a scholarship is an important opportunity.  
Education gives students better opportunities.

---

## Mistake 7: singular/plural noun mistakes

### Wrong
student can study  
student get better jobs  
private car  
digital platform

### Correct
students can study  
students get better jobs  
private cars  
digital platforms

### Rule
When speaking generally, use plural nouns.

### Memorize
Students can study from home.  
Many people prefer private cars.  
Digital platforms help students attend online classes.

---

## Mistake 8: Run-on sentence

### Wrong
Education creates opportunities, it helps students get better jobs.

### Correct
Education creates opportunities because it helps students get better jobs.

### Rule
Do not join two full sentences with only a comma.

### Memorize
Education creates opportunities because it helps students get better jobs.  
Technology is useful because it helps people communicate.  
Online learning is flexible, so students can study from home.

---

## Mistake 9: should be aware

### Wrong
We should aware of that.

### Correct
We should be aware of it.

### Rule
After **should**, use base verb. Here the correct phrase is **be aware of**.

### Memorize
We should be aware of the negative effects of social media.  
Students should be aware of wasting time online.

---

## Mistake 10: from home / at home, not in home

### Wrong
Students can study in home.

### Correct
Students can study from home.  
Students can study at home.

### Memorize
Online learning allows students to study from home.  
Many students prefer studying at home.

# 5. Vocabulary Upgrade List

## Weak word: good
Better words:
- beneficial
- useful
- effective
- valuable
- helpful

Example:
Online learning is beneficial because it makes education more accessible.

---

## Weak word: bad
Better words:
- harmful
- negative
- detrimental
- problematic

Example:
Excessive social media use can be harmful because it reduces concentration.

---

## Weak word: many
Better words:
- numerous
- a large number of
- several
- a considerable number of

Example:
A large number of students use digital platforms for learning.

---

## Weak phrase: get better job
Better phrase:
- get better jobs
- find better employment opportunities
- improve their career prospects

Example:
Education helps students improve their career prospects.

---

## Weak phrase: study from home
Better phrase:
- attend classes from home
- study remotely
- access education from home

Example:
Students can attend classes from home through digital platforms.

# 6. IELTS Task 1 Memorization

## Overview formula
Overall, + highest item + was/were the most popular/dominant, while + lowest item + was/were the least popular/preferred.  
In addition, + middle item + accounted for a smaller/larger proportion than + comparison item.

## Model Overview
Overall, online classes were clearly the dominant learning method, while library study was the least preferred option. In addition, private tutoring accounted for a considerably smaller proportion than online classes.

## Useful Task 1 Words
- dominant
- least preferred
- accounted for
- proportion
- percentage
- figure
- considerably
- slightly
- whereas
- compared with

# 7. IELTS Task 2 Memorization

## Body paragraph formula
Topic sentence + Explanation + Example + Result

## Model Paragraph
Online learning is more useful because it makes education more flexible and accessible. Students can attend classes from home, save travel time, and review lessons whenever they need. For example, learners who live in rural areas can study with qualified teachers through digital platforms. This is especially helpful for students who do not have access to good schools or private tutors. As a result, online learning gives students more opportunities to study effectively and manage their time conveniently.

## Useful Task 2 Phrases
- makes education more flexible and accessible
- save travel time
- review lessons whenever they need
- qualified teachers
- digital platforms
- rural areas
- educational opportunities
- manage their time conveniently
- face-to-face communication
- academic performance

# 11. Final Checklist Before Writing

Before submitting IELTS practice, check:

- Did I start every sentence with a capital letter?
- Did I end every sentence with a full stop?
- Did I avoid **although + but**?
- Did I use **helps + object + base verb**?
- Did I use **opportunity to + verb**?
- Did I use **affect** correctly as a verb?
- Did I use **students'** for possession?
- Did I check spelling of beneficial, because, students, communication, opportunity?
- Did I avoid long unclear sentences?
- Did I use **As a result** in Task 2 instead of **Overall**?
