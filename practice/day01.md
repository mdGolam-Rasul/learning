## 🔴 PART 2: Grammar Rules

### Rule 1: Adjective needs `am/is/are`
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct |
|---------|-----------|
| I tired | I **am** tired |
| I happy | I **am** happy |
| He poor | He **is** poor |

**Rule:** Adjective er age ALWAYS am/is/are!

---

### Rule 2: No `will` after `if`
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct |
|---------|-----------|
| If I will practice | If I **practice** |
| If I will get time | If I **get** time |

**Formula:** `If + present simple, + will + verb`

---

### Rule 3: `make` a mistake (not `do`)
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct |
|---------|-----------|
| I do mistake | I **make** mistakes |

---

### Rule 4: success / succeed / successful
| Form | Type | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| success | noun | It was a success |
| succeed | verb | I will succeed |
| successful | adjective | I will be successful |

---

### Rule 5: Correct Prepositions
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct |
|---------|-----------|
| angry **to** me | angry **with** me |
| necessary **to** me | necessary **for** me |
| **at** early childhood | **in** early childhood |
| **for** young age | **at** a young age |
| mastering **in** English | mastering English |

---

### Rule 6: `help` structure
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct |
|---------|-----------|
| helps to getting a job | helps **people get** a job |

**Rule:** help + someone + verb (base form)

---

### Rule 7: `facilitate` + noun
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct |
|---------|-----------|
| facilitates to communicate | facilitates **communication** |

---

### Rule 8: Don't mix `while` + `however`
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct |
|---------|-----------|
| While X, however Y | While X, Y |

Pick one, not both!

---

### Rule 9: `to + verb` for purpose
| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct |
|---------|-----------|
| I go to school **for learn** | I go to school **to learn** |

---

### Rule 10: Present vs Adjective vs Present Perfect
| Type | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Present Simple | S + verb | I practice every day |
| Adjective state | S + am/is/are + adj | I am tired |
| Present Perfect | S + have/has + V3 | I have learned a lot |

## 🟢 PART 3: `Would` Use Cases

| Use | Structure | Example |
|-----|-----------|---------|
| Polite wish | would like to + verb | I would like to learn |
| 2nd Conditional | If + past, would + verb | If I had time, I would practice |
| 3rd Conditional | If + had + V3, would have + V3 | If I had had time, I would have practiced |
| Past habit | would + verb | When young, I would play cricket |

## 🔷 PART 7: IELTS Intro Templates

### Template A: Opinion Essay
> "In recent years, [TOPIC] has become extremely crucial for individuals worldwide. While some people argue that [OPPOSITE VIEW], I firmly believe that [YOUR VIEW] because [REASON 1] and [REASON 2]."

### Template B: Discuss Both Views
> "In recent years, [TOPIC] has become a widely debated issue. While some people believe that [VIEW A], others argue that [VIEW B]. In this essay, I will discuss both perspectives before giving my opinion that [YOUR OPINION]."

### Template C: Advantages / Disadvantages
> "In contemporary society, [TOPIC] has gained significant attention. Although this trend has several advantages, I believe the drawbacks are more significant, which will be discussed in this essay."

## 📘 PART 8: Body Paragraph — PEEL Method

| Letter | Meaning | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **P** | Point | Firstly, English enhances career opportunities. |
| **E** | Explain | Most multinational companies require English skills. |
| **E** | Example | For instance, Google and Microsoft prioritize English. |
| **L** | Link | Therefore, English is essential for jobs. |
