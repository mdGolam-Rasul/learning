## 🎯 PART 1: 7 STEPS FORMULA — QUICK SUMMARY

**STEP 1:** Subject + Verb + Object  
→ The boy runs.

**STEP 2:** + Adjective (before noun)  
→ The tall boy runs.

**STEP 3:** + Adverb (with verb)  
→ The tall boy runs quickly.

**STEP 4:** + Preposition Phrase (place + time)  
→ The tall boy runs quickly in the park every morning.

**STEP 5:** + Conjunction (and/but/because/so/when)  
→ ...because he wants to stay healthy.

**STEP 6:** + Relative Clause (who/which/that)  
→ The tall boy who lives next door runs...

**STEP 7:** + Participle Phrase (-ing / -ed)  
→ Wearing sports shoes, the tall boy...

## ⚠️ PART 2: COMMON MISTAKES — REVIEW

### 🔴 Mistake 1: Subject-Verb Agreement

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|--------|--------|
| He love music | He loves music |
| Rain fall heavily | Rain falls heavily |
| She study hard | She studies hard |
| Birds flies | Birds fly |

**Rule:**  
- I / You / We / They → base verb  
- He / She / It → verb + s/es  

---

### 🔴 Mistake 2: "to" + Base Verb

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|--------|--------|
| to teaching | to teach |
| to reading | to read |
| to going | to go |

**Rule:**  
👉 "to" + base verb (NO -ing)

---

### 🔴 Mistake 3: Adjective vs Noun

| Noun | Adjective |
|------|----------|
| talent | talented |
| beauty | beautiful |
| success | successful |

❌ A talent girl  
✅ A talented girl  

---

### 🔴 Mistake 4: Articles (a/an/the)

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Right |
|--------|--------|
| He is good teacher | He is a good teacher |
| I ate mango | I ate a mango |

**Rules:**  
- a → consonant sound  
- an → vowel sound  
- the → specific  

---

### 🔴 Mistake 5: Pronoun

❌ A girl and his father  
✅ A girl and her father  

---

### 🔴 Mistake 6: who / which / that

| Word | Use |
|------|-----|
| who | person |
| which | thing |
| that | both |

---

### 🔴 Mistake 7: Double Verb

❌ who is runs  
✅ who runs  

---

### 🔴 Mistake 8: -ing vs -ed

| Thing | Person |
|------|--------|
| boring | bored |
| exciting | excited |

---

### 🔴 Mistake 9: Preposition

| Use | Example |
|-----|--------|
| in | in the room |
| on | on the table |
| at | at 5 PM |

---

### 🔴 Mistake 10: Logic

- Because → Result + Reason  
- So → Reason + Result  
- When → Event → Result
