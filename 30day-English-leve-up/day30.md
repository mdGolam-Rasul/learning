## 🧠 Test Day Strategy

### Night Before IELTS

- [ ] Light vocabulary review
- [ ] No intense studying
- [ ] No full practice test
- [ ] Light dinner
- [ ] Sleep 8 hours
- [ ] Prepare documents and materials

### Test Day Morning

- [ ] Wake up 3 hours before the test
- [ ] Eat a good breakfast
- [ ] Review vocabulary briefly
- [ ] Do 5 minutes of English speaking warm-up
- [ ] Arrive 30 minutes early
- [ ] Stay calm and breathe deeply

---

## 📝 During the Test

### Listening

- Read instructions carefully
- Write answers while listening
- Use transfer time wisely
- Check spelling and plural forms

### Reading

- Skim first
- Scan for details
- Do not spend too long on one question
- Mark difficult questions and return later

### Writing

- Task 1: 20 minutes
- Task 2: 40 minutes
- Plan before writing
- Proofread before submission

### Speaking

- Smile and maintain eye contact
- Extend answers naturally
- Use fluency fillers
- Show personality and confidence

## 🚨 Emergency Strategies

### If You Blank During Speaking

Use fillers:

```text
That's an interesting question. Let me think for a moment...
```

Then:

- Speak from personal experience
- Keep the answer simple
- Continue with confidence

### If You Get Stuck in Writing

- Use memorized structures
- Start with the easiest part
- Focus on completion, not perfection
- Leave time to proofread

### General Strategy

- Take a deep breath
- Trust your preparation
- Remember your mock test experience
- Stay calm and continue
