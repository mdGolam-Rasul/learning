# 📚 Section 3: Grammar Mastery Check

## Tenses Test

Fill in the blanks.

```text
1. Simple Present:
I _______ (study) English every day.

2. Present Continuous:
I _______ (study) for IELTS right now.

3. Present Perfect:
I _______ (study) English for 3 years.

4. Present Perfect Continuous:
I _______ (study) since morning.

5. Simple Past:
Yesterday, I _______ (go) to the library.

6. Past Continuous:
I _______ (read) when the phone rang.

7. Past Perfect:
Before the exam, I _______ (complete) all chapters.

8. Simple Future:
Tomorrow, I _______ (take) the mock test.

9. Future Continuous:
This time next week, I _______ (prepare) for exams.

10. Conditional Type 2:
If I _______ (have) more time, I _______ (travel) abroad.
```

## Answer Key

```text
1. study
2. am studying
3. have studied
4. have been studying
5. went
6. was reading
7. had completed
8. will take
9. will be preparing
10. had / would travel
```
