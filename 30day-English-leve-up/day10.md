# 🎤 12 Common Part 1 Hobbies Questions

## Question 1

**What are your hobbies?**

### Band 7+ Answer

I have several hobbies that I am genuinely passionate about. Primarily, I am an avid reader, and I particularly relish fiction novels that transport me to different worlds. Additionally, I am a photography enthusiast, which I find absolutely captivating. These pastimes help me unwind and stay creatively engaged.

---

## Question 2

**How did you become interested in this hobby?**

### Band 7+ Answer

It all started during my childhood years. I vividly remember my father gifting me my first novel when I was eight. From that moment, I was completely hooked. Over the years, my passion for reading has evolved, and now I indulge in various genres, from classic literature to contemporary fiction.

---

## Question 3

**How often do you do this hobby?**

### Band 7+ Answer

I try to dedicate time to reading on a daily basis. On weekdays, I usually read for about an hour before bed to unwind. On weekends, I immerse myself in books for several hours. Ideally, I aim to finish at least two books per month, although my hectic schedule sometimes makes this challenging.

---

## Question 4

**Is this hobby popular in your country?**

### Band 7+ Answer

It is a mixed picture. Traditional reading has somewhat declined among younger generations due to the influence of digital entertainment. However, there has been a noticeable resurgence in reading culture, particularly with book clubs and literary festivals gaining momentum. The Ekushey Book Fair in Dhaka attracts millions of book lovers.

---

## Question 5

**Do you think hobbies are important? Why?**

### Band 7+ Answer

Absolutely. Hobbies play a pivotal role in our lives. Firstly, they help us unwind after hectic days and alleviate stress significantly. Secondly, they foster creativity and enhance cognitive abilities. Moreover, hobbies broaden our horizons and allow us to discover hidden talents. Essentially, they enrich our overall well-being.

---

## Question 6

**Did you have different hobbies when you were younger?**

### Band 7+ Answer

Yes, definitely. As a child, I was particularly fond of playing cricket with my neighborhood friends. I also used to indulge in collecting stamps, which I found genuinely fascinating. However, as I grew older, my interests evolved. Nowadays, I prefer more intellectual pursuits like reading and photography.

---

## Question 7

**Do you prefer indoor or outdoor hobbies?**

### Band 7+ Answer

Honestly, I enjoy both types, but I have a slight preference for outdoor activities. There is something incredibly refreshing about engaging with nature through photography walks or morning jogs. That said, indoor hobbies like reading offer their own unique charm and are perfect for rainy days.

---

## Question 8

**What new hobby would you like to try?**

### Band 7+ Answer

There are several hobbies I have been eager to explore. Primarily, I would love to take up painting, which I believe would be incredibly therapeutic and would help me express my creativity. Additionally, I am genuinely interested in learning to play the guitar. If I had more free time, I would definitely pursue these passions.

---

## Question 9

**Do you think children spend enough time on hobbies these days?**

### Band 7+ Answer

Unfortunately, no. Modern children tend to be overly absorbed in digital devices, which negatively affects their time for traditional hobbies. Academic pressure also leaves them with little free time. In my opinion, parents should encourage children to cultivate diverse hobbies because these activities foster creativity and overall development.

---

## Question 10

**Do you share your hobbies with friends or family?**

### Band 7+ Answer

Yes, absolutely. Sharing hobbies strengthens bonds and creates cherished memories. For instance, I frequently discuss books with my sister, who is equally passionate about literature. Additionally, my friends and I occasionally organize photography trips, which are incredibly enjoyable. Sharing hobbies makes them even more fulfilling.

---

## Question 11

**Can hobbies help you in your career?**

### Band 7+ Answer

Definitely. Hobbies can significantly enhance one’s career. Firstly, they develop transferable skills like creativity, discipline, and problem-solving. For example, my photography hobby has sharpened my attention to detail, which is invaluable in my field. Furthermore, hobbies can create networking opportunities and even open up new career paths.

---

## Question 12

**Do you spend money on your hobbies?**

### Band 7+ Answer

Yes, I do invest in my hobbies, though reasonably. For reading, I occasionally purchase new books or subscribe to literary magazines. For photography, I have gradually invested in decent equipment. However, I believe enjoying hobbies should not be extremely expensive. Passion and dedication matter more than costly gear.

# 📚 Section 3: Story Twists & Surprises

## What Is a Story Twist?

A twist is an unexpected moment that makes a story more memorable.

Twists are useful because they:

- Keep the listener engaged
- Make stories more creative
- Create emotional impact
- Show advanced storytelling ability
- Make simple experiences sound interesting

---

# 🎯 5 Types of Story Twists

## 1. Unexpected Event

### Simple Version

```text
I went to the park. I had a good time.
```

### With Twist

```text
I went to the park expecting a quiet afternoon, but out of nowhere, I bumped into my childhood friend whom I had not seen for over a decade.
```

---

## 2. Surprising Discovery

### Simple Version

```text
I was cleaning my room. I found old things.
```

### With Twist

```text
While cleaning my old room, I stumbled upon a dusty box. To my astonishment, inside were love letters my grandfather had written to my grandmother during the war.
```

---

## 3. Emotional Reveal

### Simple Version

```text
My father gave me a gift. I was happy.
```

### With Twist

```text
My father handed me a small package with tears in his eyes. Little did I know that inside was my late grandmother’s necklace, which she had asked him to give me on my graduation day.
```

---

## 4. Coincidence

### Simple Version

```text
I met someone at the airport. They were nice.
```

### With Twist

```text
As I was waiting for my flight, I started talking with a friendly stranger. Incredibly, we discovered that we were going to the same conference and had even applied for the same job.
```

---

## 5. Plot Reversal

### Simple Version

```text
I was scared but everything was fine.
```

### With Twist

```text
My heart pounded as I approached the dark room, expecting the worst. But to my relief, when I switched on the light, my entire family shouted, “Surprise!” They had organized a secret birthday party for me.
```

---

# 🔑 Twist Signal Phrases

| Purpose | Phrases |
|---|---|
| Introducing twist | Suddenly, Out of nowhere, Unexpectedly, All of a sudden |
| Surprise discovery | To my astonishment, To my utter surprise, Much to my shock |
| Revealing information | Little did I know, What I did not realize was, Unbeknownst to me |
| Coincidence | Incredibly, Remarkably, As luck would have it |
| Plot reversal | However, Contrary to my expectations, Against all odds |

---

# 🎨 Twist Building Formula

```text
Setup:
I was doing something normal.

Build-up:
Something ordinary was happening.

Twist signal:
Suddenly / Out of nowhere / Unexpectedly...

Revelation:
A surprising event happens.

Emotional reaction:
I felt shocked, excited, or overwhelmed as I realized its meaning.
```

---

# 🔥 Complete Story With Twist Example

## Setup + Build-Up

Last summer, I embarked on a routine family trip to Cox’s Bazar. The weather was absolutely gorgeous, and we were strolling leisurely along the picturesque beach, enjoying the serene atmosphere.

## Twist Signal + Revelation

Suddenly, my younger sister let out an excited shout. Much to our astonishment, she had spotted a rare sea turtle slowly making its way to the ocean.

## Emotional Reaction

# ✍️ Section 7: Writing Task 1 Process Diagram

## What Is a Process Diagram?

A process diagram shows how something is made or how something happens.

---

## Process Description Rules

- Use passive voice
- Use sequence words
- Describe stages in order
- Use Present Simple tense
- No numbers are usually needed

---

# 🔁 Sequence Vocabulary

| Stage | Useful Words |
|---|---|
| Beginning | Firstly, Initially, To begin with, The process starts with |
| Middle | Secondly, Subsequently, Next, Following this, After that |
| Continuation | Then, At this stage, Meanwhile |
| End | Finally, Ultimately, Lastly, In the final stage |

---

# 🧱 Passive Voice Examples

| Active | Passive |
|---|---|
| Farmers grow rice. | Rice is grown by farmers. |
| The machine processes materials. | Materials are processed by the machine. |
| Workers package products. | Products are packaged by workers. |

---

# 📝 Sample Process Task 1

## Question

```text
The diagram below shows the process of making paper from wood.
```

## Stages

```text
1. Trees are cut.
2. Wood is chopped into small pieces.
3. Pieces are mixed with water and chemicals.
4. Mixture is pressed into sheets.
5. Sheets are dried.
6. Final paper is rolled for distribution.
```

## Sample Introduction

The diagram illustrates the various stages involved in producing paper from wood, consisting of six distinct steps from raw material to final product.

## Sample Overview

Overall, the process begins with the harvesting of trees and ends with the distribution of finished paper, involving both mechanical and chemical processes.

## Sample Body Paragraph

Firstly, mature trees are cut down and transported to the factory. Subsequently, the wood is chopped into smaller pieces to facilitate processing. At the next stage, these wood chips are mixed with water and chemicals to form a pulp.
