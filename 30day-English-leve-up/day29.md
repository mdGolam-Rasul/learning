# 🌅 Morning Block: Final Speaking Mock

## 🎤 Speaking Part 1 — Introduction

**Duration:** 4–5 minutes  
**Instruction:** Record yourself answering each question for 30–45 seconds.

### Questions

1. Can you tell me your full name and where you're from?
2. Do you work or study? Tell me about it.
3. What do you find most rewarding about your work or studies?
4. How do you usually spend your free time?
5. Do you prefer reading books or watching movies?
6. What kind of music do you enjoy?
7. Are you more of an outdoor or indoor person?
8. Do you enjoy learning new things? What have you learned recently?
9. How important is family in your life?
10. What are you most looking forward to in the coming year?

---

## 🎤 Speaking Part 2 — Cue Card

**Preparation:** 1 minute  
**Speaking Time:** 2 minutes  

### Cue Card

Describe an important decision you made in your life.

You should say:

- What the decision was
- When you made it
- Why you made that decision
- How it changed your life

---

## 🎤 Speaking Part 3 — Discussion

**Duration:** 4–5 minutes  
**Instruction:** Answer each question for 45–60 seconds.

### Questions

1. Why do some people find it difficult to make important decisions?
2. How do young people's decision-making skills differ from older generations?
3. Should parents influence their children's major life decisions?
4. How has technology changed the way people make decisions today?

# 🌙 Writing Mock

## ✍️ Writing Task 1

**Time Limit:** 20 minutes  
**Minimum Words:** 150 words  

### Question

The line graph below shows the number of students enrolled in three different types of higher education programs in Bangladesh from 2015 to 2025:

- Traditional University
- Online Learning
- Vocational Training

### Data

| Year | Traditional University | Online Learning | Vocational Training |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 2015 | 200 | 20 | 80 |
| 2018 | 220 | 60 | 100 |
| 2020 | 180 | 200 | 110 |
| 2023 | 190 | 250 | 140 |
| 2025 | 210 | 320 | 180 |

> Data is shown in thousands.

---

## 📝 Task 1 Structure

| Paragraph | Purpose | Suggested Length |
|---|---|---:|
| Introduction | Paraphrase the question | 25 words |
| Overview | Main trends without numbers | 30 words |
| Body 1 | Most dramatic change: online learning | 50 words |
| Body 2 | Traditional and vocational details | 45 words |

---

## 🎯 Key Trends to Notice

- Online learning increased dramatically from **20k to 320k**
- Traditional university enrollment fluctuated but slightly increased overall
- Vocational training showed steady growth
- 2020 was a pivotal year, likely influenced by online learning growth

---

## ✍️ Writing Task 2

**Time Limit:** 40 minutes  
**Minimum Words:** 250 words  

### Question

> In many countries, young people are increasingly interested in starting their own businesses rather than working for established companies. What are the reasons for this trend, and is it a positive or negative development?

---

## 📝 Task 2 Structure

| Paragraph | Purpose | Suggested Length |
|---|---|---:|
| Introduction | Paraphrase + preview answers | 50 words |
| Body 1 | Reasons for the trend | 90 words |
| Body 2 | Positive or negative development | 90 words |
| Conclusion | Summary + final opinion | 40 words |

---

## 💡 Task 2 Key Ideas

### Reasons

- Technology enables entrepreneurship through e-commerce, apps, and digital platforms
- Young people want flexibility and independence
- Startups can offer lucrative opportunities
- Many are dissatisfied with traditional career paths
- Success stories inspire young entrepreneurs

### Positive Aspects

- Innovation
- Economic growth
- Job creation
- Personal fulfillment
- Technological advancement

### Negative Aspects

- High failure rate
- Financial insecurity
- Stress and burnout
- Lack of stability

### Suggested Position

This trend is **mostly positive** if young people are well-prepared.
