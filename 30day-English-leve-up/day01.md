# 📚 Section 1: Grammar Foundation

## ✅ Simple Present Tense

### Use

Simple Present Tense is used for:

- Daily routine
- General truth
- Habits
- Repeated actions

### Formula

```text
Subject + Verb + Object
```

For `he / she / it`, use verb + `s/es`.

| Subject | Verb Form | Example |
|---|---|---|
| I / You / We / They | Base verb | I go to school. |
| He / She / It | Verb + s/es | She goes to school. |

### Examples

```text
I study English every day.
She works at a hospital.
They play cricket on weekends.
My father reads newspapers every morning.
Water boils at 100°C.
```

---

## ✅ Simple Past Tense

### Use

Simple Past Tense is used for actions that finished in the past.

### Formula

```text
Subject + Past Verb + Object
```

### Examples

```text
Yesterday, I went to the market.
She ate rice at 2 PM.
They played football last weekend.
I saw a beautiful bird.
```

# 🎬 Section 3: Storytelling Structure

## 🌟 The 5-Part Story Structure

```text
1️⃣ Setting
   Where and when did it happen?

2️⃣ Character
   Who was involved?

3️⃣ Event / Problem
   What happened?

4️⃣ Action
   What did the character do?

5️⃣ Resolution + Lesson
   How did it end? What was learned?
```

---

## 🎨 Story Expansion Practice

### Simple Story

```text
A boy went to school. He studied. He was happy.
```

### Step 1: Add Setting

```text
One bright Monday morning, a boy went to school. He studied. He was happy.
```

### Step 2: Add Character Details

```text
One bright Monday morning, a young boy named Rakib, who lived in a small village, went to school for the first time. He studied. He was happy.
```

### Step 3: Add Event + Emotion

```text
One bright Monday morning, a young boy named Rakib, who lived in a small village, went to school for the first time. Feeling a mix of excitement and nervousness,
 he met his new teacher. He studied hard throughout the day. He was happy.
```

### Step 4: Add Action + Rich Vocabulary

```text
One bright Monday morning, a young boy named Rakib, who lived in a small village, embarked on his first day of school. Feeling a mix of excitement and nervousness,
he met his diligent teacher, who instilled confidence in him. He enthusiastically studied throughout the day, eager to acquire knowledge. He was happy.
```

---

## 🏆 Final Band 7+ Story

```text
One bright Monday morning in a small village in Bangladesh, a young boy named Rakib embarked on his first day of school, carrying a small bag and big dreams. Feeling a mix of
 excitement and nervousness, he stepped into the classroom, where his diligent teacher warmly welcomed him and instilled confidence in his nervous heart. Throughout the day,
 he enthusiastically participated in every lesson, eager to acquire knowledge and broaden his horizons. By the end of the day, Rakib returned home with a heart full of joy, realizing that school
 would shape his future.This profound experience taught him that education is the first step toward achieving one's aspirations.
```

# 🎤 Section 5: Speaking Practice

## Task: 2-Minute Self-Introduction

### Template

```text
[GREETING]
Hello, my name is _______, and I'm absolutely delighted to introduce myself.

[BASIC INFO]
I come from _______, a _______ city/town in Bangladesh. I am currently _______.

[EDUCATION]
I'm pursuing my _______ at _______. My academic discipline is _______, which I'm genuinely passionate about because _______.

[INTERESTS]
In my free time, I thoroughly enjoy _______. It helps me unwind and broaden my horizons.

[FAMILY]
I come from a closely-knit family of _______. My parents have instilled strong values in me.

[ASPIRATIONS]
My career aspiration is to become _______, and I'm diligently working towards it.
```

---

## Sample Band 7+ Introduction

```text
Hello, my name is Ahmed, and I'm absolutely delighted to introduce myself today. I come from Dhaka, the bustling capital of Bangladesh, where I have lived all my life.

I am currently pursuing my undergraduate studies in Computer Science at a prestigious institution, which I find incredibly stimulating.
 I am genuinely passionate about programming because it enhances my problem-solving skills and fosters creativity.

In my free time, I thoroughly enjoy reading novels and playing cricket, which help me unwind after demanding study sessions. Reading, in particular,
 has broadened my horizons and enriched my understanding of different cultures.

I come from a closely-knit family of four. My parents, who are my biggest inspiration, have instilled core values such as honesty and diligence in me.

My career aspiration is to become a software engineer and contribute meaningfully to the tech industry.
```
