# 🌅 Morning Block: Speaking Mock

## 🎤 Section 1: Speaking Mock Test 4

**Duration:** 15 minutes  
**Instruction:** Record yourself answering all speaking parts.

---

## Part 1: Introduction

**Duration:** 4–5 minutes  
**Answer Length:** 30–45 seconds per answer

### Questions

1. Can you tell me about your current job or studies?
2. What do you enjoy most about it?
3. Do you think you will continue in this field?
4. How important is work-life balance to you?
5. What is your ideal weekend?
6. Do you prefer spending time alone or with others?
7. What is a skill you would like to develop?
8. How do you stay healthy?
9. What role does technology play in your daily life?
10. What are your ambitions for the next 5 years?

---

## Part 2: Cue Card

**Preparation Time:** 1 minute  
**Speaking Time:** 2 minutes  

### Cue Card

Describe a person who has had a positive influence on your life.

You should say:

- Who this person is
- How you know them
- What they have taught you
- Why they have been influential

---

## Part 3: Discussion

**Duration:** 4–5 minutes  
**Answer Length:** 30–60 seconds each

### Questions

1. How do you think family relationships have changed in recent years?
2. What qualities make someone a good role model?
3. Do you think people are influenced more by family or by society?
4. How can we encourage young people to make positive life choices?
