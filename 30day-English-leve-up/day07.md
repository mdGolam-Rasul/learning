# 📚 Section 1: Grammar Complete Review

## Your 6-Day Grammar Journey

### ✅ 9 Tenses Mastered

| Tense | Formula | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Simple Present | Subject + V1 / V1+s/es | I study English. |
| Simple Past | Subject + V2 | I studied yesterday. |
| Simple Future | Subject + will + V1 | I will study tomorrow. |
| Present Continuous | am / is / are + V-ing | I am studying now. |
| Past Continuous | was / were + V-ing | I was studying at 8 PM. |
| Future Continuous | will be + V-ing | I will be studying tomorrow. |
| Present Perfect | have / has + V3 | I have studied for hours. |
| Past Perfect | had + V3 | I had studied before the test. |
| Present Perfect Continuous | have / has been + V-ing | I have been studying for hours. |

---

## ✅ Conditionals

| Type | Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Type 1 | Real future | If I study, I will pass. |
| Type 2 | Imaginary present | If I had money, I would travel. |
| Type 3 | Imaginary past | If I had studied, I would have passed. |

---

- Relative clauses: who, whom, whose, which, that, where, when
- Participles: present, past, perfect
- Articles: a, an, the
- Prepositions: in, on, at

## 🎯 Quick Grammar Quiz

Fix the errors in these sentences.

```text
1. I am doing the MBBS in University of Dhaka since 2023.

2. Yesterday, I am going to market and buy vegetables.

3. If I will have time, I would help you.

4. The boy which is standing there is my brother.

5. Having eaten lunch, I was going to park.
```

---

## ✅ Answer Key

```text
1. I have been doing an MBBS at the University of Dhaka since 2023.

2. Yesterday, I went to the market and bought vegetables.

3. If I have time, I will help you.
   OR
   If I had time, I would help you.

4. The boy who is standing there is my brother.

5. Having eaten lunch, I went to the park.
```

# 📚 Section 3: Storytelling Techniques Review

## 4 Expansion Techniques Mastered

---

## 🎨 1. Setting Expansion

### Simple

```text
It was morning. I was at home.
```

### Rich

```text
It was a glorious spring morning, with golden sunlight streaming through my windows and a refreshing breeze gently swaying the curtains. I was lounging comfortably on my sofa.
```

---

## 👤 2. Character Expansion

### Simple

```text
My mother is kind.
```

### Rich

```text
My mother, a compassionate woman in her late forties with gentle brown eyes and a warm smile, is incredibly kind and has sacrificed countless things to nurture our family.
```

---

## 🏃 3. Action Expansion

### Simple

```text
She ran.
```

### Rich

```text
With her heart pounding furiously, she sprinted through the bustling streets, desperately trying to catch the departing bus.
```

---

## 💖 4. Emotion Expansion

### Simple

```text
I was happy.
```

### Rich

```text
An overwhelming wave of pure joy washed over me. Tears of happiness welled up in my eyes, and a radiant smile spread across my face. I felt as though I was walking on clouds.
```

---

## Task 1 Checklist

- [ ] Introduction paraphrases the question
- [ ] Overview has no numbers
- [ ] Body 1 covers highest or most significant data
- [ ] Body 2 covers remaining data with comparisons
- [ ] Trend vocabulary is used
- [ ] 150+ words written
- [ ] Finished in 20 minutes

---

## Task 2 Preview

**Time:** 20 minutes  
**Minimum:** 250 words

### Question

Many people believe that spending time with family is more important than earning money. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

---

## Simple Task 2 Structure

| Paragraph | Purpose | Suggested Length |
|---|---|---:|
| Paragraph 1 | Introduction: background + paraphrase + opinion | 50 words |
| Paragraph 2 | Body 1: main reason + example | 100 words |
| Paragraph 3 | Body 2: counter view or additional support | 60 words |
| Paragraph 4 | Conclusion: summary + restate opinion | 40 words |

---

## Sample Band 7+ Introduction

```text
In contemporary society, the debate between prioritizing family relationships and financial pursuits has gained significant attention. While some argue that earning money is essential, I firmly believe that spending quality time with family is far more valuable for a fulfilling life.
```
