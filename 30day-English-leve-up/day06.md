# рџ“љ Section 1: Grammar вЂ” Relative Clauses + Participles

## рџ”Ґ Relative Clauses вЂ” The Band 7+ Game Changer

Relative clauses help you combine two simple sentences into one sophisticated sentence.

---

## вњ… Relative Pronouns

| Pronoun | Use For | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **who** | People as subject | The boy who is standing there is my brother. |
| **whom** | People as object | The teacher whom I met was kind. |
| **whose** | Possession | The girl whose bag is red is my sister. |
| **which** | Things / animals | The book which I bought is interesting. |
| **that** | People or things | The car that I want is expensive. |
| **where** | Places | This is the school where I studied. |
| **when** | Time | I remember the day when we met. |

---

## вњ… Basic Examples

### Example 1

```text
Two sentences:
I have a friend. He lives in Dhaka.

Combined:
I have a friend who lives in Dhaka.
```

### Example 2

```text
Two sentences:
I bought a book. It was expensive.

Combined:
I bought a book which was expensive.
```

### Example 3

```text
Two sentences:
This is the house. I grew up here.

Combined:
This is the house where I grew up.
```

---

## рџ”Ґ Two Types of Relative Clauses

## Type 1: Defining Relative Clause

Defining relative clauses give essential information.  
Do **not** use commas.

```text
The man who lives next door is a doctor.
The book that I bought yesterday is interesting.
```

---

## Type 2: Non-Defining Relative Clause

Non-defining relative clauses give extra information.  
Use commas.

```text
My father, who is a teacher, is very kind.
Dhaka, which is the capital of Bangladesh, is very crowded.
```

---

## рџ’Ћ Band 7+ Complex Sentences

### Band 7+


# вњЁ Participles вЂ” Sentence Combining Magic

Participles help combine sentences smoothly and make your writing more advanced.

---

## вњ… Present Participle

### Structure

```text
Verb + ing
```

### Use

Present participle shows active meaning or simultaneous action.

### Examples

```text
Two sentences:
I was walking in the park. I saw a bird.

Combined:
Walking in the park, I saw a bird.
```

```text
Two sentences:
She was feeling happy. She smiled.

Combined:
Feeling happy, she smiled.
```

---

## вњ… Past Participle

### Structure

```text
Verb-ed / V3
```

### Use

Past participle often shows passive meaning or completed state.

### Examples

```text
Two sentences:
I was tired from work. I went to bed early.

Combined:
Tired from work, I went to bed early.
```

```text
Two sentences:
She was exhausted after running. She collapsed on the sofa.

Combined:
Exhausted after running, she collapsed on the sofa.
```

---

## вњ… Perfect Participle

### Structure

```text
Having + Past Participle V3
```

### Use

Perfect participle shows one action happened before another action.

### Examples

```text
Two sentences:
I had finished my homework. I went out to play.

Combined:
Having finished my homework, I went out to play.
```

```text
Two sentences:
She had worked hard all day. She decided to rest.

Combined:
Having worked hard all day, she decided to rest.
```

---

## рџЋЇ Mega Combination вЂ” Band 8+ Magic

### Simple Version

```text

She is 75 years old.
She lives in the village.
She tells amazing stories.
I visit her every summer.
```

### Band 8+ Version


# рџЋ¤ Section 5: Cue Card вЂ” Family Event

## IELTS Speaking Part 2 Cue Card

Describe a memorable family event.

You should say:

```text
- What the event was
- When and where it took place
- Who was there
- Why it was memorable
```

Time:

```text
1 minute preparation + 2 minutes speaking
```

---

## Band 7+ Template

```text
[INTRODUCTION]
I'd like to share a truly heartwarming family event that happened [when] in [where]. It's a memory I will cherish forever.

[SETTING]
At the time, [describe the atmosphere, weather, mood]. Our closely-knit family, which had gathered to celebrate [occasion], was feeling [emotion].

[EVENT]
The event was a [type of celebration]. My [family member], who is [description], was [action]. While we were [action], suddenly [something happened]. I was standing next to [person], feeling [emotion].

[EMOTION / CLIMAX]
The most memorable moment was when [climax]. An overwhelming wave of [emotion] washed over me. Everyone's eyes were [reaction]. It felt as though [metaphor].

[CONCLUSION]
This event has had a profound impact on me. It taught me the value of [lesson]. I will treasure this memory forever.
```

---

## Sample Band 7+ Answer

```text

Tears of happiness welled up in her eyes, and she whispered, вЂњThis is the happiest day of my life.вЂќ It felt as though time had stopped for a beautiful moment.

This event has had a profound impact on me. It taught me the value of cherishing our loved ones and nurturing family bonds. I will treasure this memory forever.
```
