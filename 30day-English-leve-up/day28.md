# 📚 Section 1: Most Common Band 6.5 → 7+ Errors

## Error 1: Article Misuse

Articles are one of the most common mistakes for Bangladeshi IELTS candidates.

### ❌ Wrong

- I am doing MBBS in Dhaka University.
- She is good in English.
- I went to the school.
- He has the car.

### ✅ Correct

- I am doing **an MBBS at** Dhaka University.
- She is good **at** English.
- I went to school.
- He has **a** car.

### Quick Rules

| Article | Use |
|---|---|
| a / an | First mention or non-specific thing |
| the | Specific, known, or superlative |
| no article | General concepts, abstract nouns, names |

---

## Error 2: Preposition Errors

### ❌ Wrong

- I am good in mathematics.
- I live in Dhaka since 2020.
- He arrived to home.
- I am interested about music.
- Different than my brother.

### ✅ Correct

- I am good **at** mathematics.
- I have lived in Dhaka **since** 2020.
- He arrived **at** home.
- I am interested **in** music.
- Different **from** my brother.

### Important Collocations

- good at
- bad at
- excellent at
- interested in
- involved in
- afraid of
- proud of
- dependent on
- focused on
- responsible for
- famous for

## Error 3: Tense Confusion

### ❌ Wrong

- Yesterday, I have gone to the mall.
- I am studying English since 2 years.
- When I was eating, my friend come.
- If I will have time, I will help.

### ✅ Correct

- Yesterday, I went to the mall.
- I have been studying English for 2 years.
- When I was eating, my friend came.
- If I have time, I will help.

---

## Error 4: Subject-Verb Agreement

### ❌ Wrong

- He have a car.
- The book were interesting.
- Everyone are happy.
- My family are living in Dhaka.

### ✅ Correct

- He has a car.
- The book was interesting.
- Everyone is happy.
- My family is living in Dhaka.

---

## Error 5: Plural and Countable Noun Mistakes

### ❌ Wrong

- I have many informations.
- Three researchs show...
- I bought a clothes.
- She gave me advices.

### ✅ Correct

- I have a lot of information.
- Three studies show...
- I bought some clothes.
- She gave me advice.

### Common Uncountable Nouns

- information
- advice
- research
- equipment
- furniture
- luggage
- knowledge
- progress
- money
- news
- traffic
- weather

---

## Error 6: Word Form Confusion

### ❌ Wrong

- The economic of Bangladesh is improving.
- She spoke with confident.
- He is a success person.
- The music was relax.

### ✅ Correct

- The economy of Bangladesh is improving.
- She spoke with confidence.
- He is a successful person.
- The music was relaxing.

---

## Error 7: Pronoun Errors

### ❌ Wrong

- Me and my friend went...
- The teacher gave the book to myself.
- Her and I are friends.

### ✅ Correct

- My friend and I went...
- The teacher gave the book to me.
- She and I are friends.

---

## Error 8: Sentence Fragment and Run-On Sentences

### ❌ Wrong

- Because I was tired.
- Which is very important.
- I was tired I slept early.

### ✅ Correct

- I went to bed early because I was tired.
- Education, which is very important, shapes society.
- I was tired, so I slept early.

---

## Error 9: Wrong Word Order

### ❌ Wrong

- I always am busy.
- She goes sometimes to the gym.
- Always I eat breakfast.

### ✅ Correct

- I am always busy.
- She sometimes goes to the gym.
- I always eat breakfast.

### Adverb Position Rule

- After **to be** verbs  
- Before other main verbs  

---

## Error 10: Overusing Simple Sentences

### ❌ Band 6 Style

I live in Dhaka. Dhaka is the capital. It is a big city. Many people live here.

### ✅ Band 7+ Style

I live in Dhaka, which is not only the bustling capital of Bangladesh but also one of the most densely populated cities globally.

### Complex Sentence Techniques

- Relative clauses
- Participles
- Not only... but also
- Although
- Despite
- Whereas

## Weakness B: Speaking Part 3 Fluency

Use the **4-Second Rule**:

1. Hear the question  
2. Count 1-2-3-4  
3. Start with: “In my perspective...”  
4. Build your answer using:  
   - Opinion  
   - Reason  
   - Example  
   - Conclusion  

### Practice

Answer 10 random Part 3 questions, 30 seconds each.

---

## Weakness C: Complex Sentence Variety

Practice these sentence types in every paragraph:

| Type | Example |
|---|---|
| Simple | Education is important. |
| Compound | Education is important, and it improves lives. |
| Complex | Education, which improves lives, is important. |
| Compound-Complex | Education, which improves lives, is important, and governments must fund it. |
