## 🌱 Topic: ENVIRONMENT

### 🔹 Step 1 — 5 Rich Words + Collocations

| # | Word | Meaning | Collocation / Usage |
|---|------|---------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Mitigate** | to reduce harm | `mitigate climate change` / `mitigate pollution` / `mitigate risks` |
| 2 | **Sustainable** | long-lasting, eco-friendly | `sustainable development` / `sustainable energy` / `sustainable lifestyle` |
| 3 | **Deplete** | to use up / reduce | `deplete natural resources` / `deplete reserves` / `deplete the ozone` |
| 4 | **Eco-friendly** | not harmful to nature | `eco-friendly products` / `eco-friendly packaging` / `eco-friendly practices` |
| 5 | **Alarming** | worrying, serious | `alarming rate` / `alarming increase` / `alarming statistics` |

### 🔹 Step 2 — 5 Semantic Sentences

```text
1. Mitigate     → Governments must take urgent steps to mitigate the effects of global warming.
2. Sustainable  → Sustainable development is essential for protecting future generations.
3. Deplete      → Human activities continue to deplete the planet's natural resources.
4. Eco-friendly → More companies are now producing eco-friendly packaging.
5. Alarming     → Air pollution in major cities is rising at an alarming rate.
```

### 🔹 Step 3 — Storytelling (Easy → Big Sentence)

**5 Easy Sentences:**
- Nadia lives in a big city.
- The air is polluted.
- She plants trees every month.
- She uses eco-friendly products.
- She wants to save nature.

**🔥 Big Band 7+ Sentence:**
> *Nadia, who lives in a polluted city where air quality has reached an alarming level, plants trees every month and uses eco-friendly products in an effort to mitigate environmental damage and promote a sustainable lifestyle, even as urban expansion continues to deplete natural resources.*

💡 **Storytelling Tip:** Add **sensory details** (what you see, smell, feel) to make your Speaking Part 2 answer vivid — a proven Band 7+ strategy.

---

### 🔹 Step 4 — 7 Sentence Structure Variants

```markdown
1. Simple            → Nadia plants trees every month.
2. Compound          → Pollution is rising, but Nadia is taking action.
3. Complex           → Although the city is polluted, Nadia continues her eco-friendly habits.
4. Compound-Complex  → Because resources are being depleted, Nadia plants trees, and she encourages others to join her.
5. Conditional       → If everyone adopted a sustainable lifestyle, pollution would decrease significantly.
6. Relative Clause   → Nadia, who is deeply concerned about the environment, uses only eco-friendly products.
7. Participle Phrase → Worried by the alarming pollution levels, Nadia decided to plant trees every month.
```

✅ **Band 7+ Rule:** Use **2–3 different structures per paragraph** to show grammatical range.

### 🔹 Step 5 — Mini Paragraph

> *Environmental degradation has become an **alarming** global issue in the 21st century. Human activities continue to **deplete** essential natural resources such as forests, clean water, and fossil fuels. To **mitigate** these harmful effects, individuals and governments must promote **sustainable** practices and invest in **eco-friendly** technologies. Simple actions — like planting trees, reducing plastic use, and conserving energy — can collectively create a powerful positive impact on our planet's future.*

📊 **Word count:** ~75 words | Uses all 5 words ✅ | Mixed structures ✅

## 🎯 Speaking Challenge

### Cue Card — Environment 🌱
> *"Describe an environmental problem in your area."*

**Structure your answer:**
1. **Where** is the problem? What does it look like?
2. **What** causes it? *(Use your 5 words!)*
3. **How** does it affect people's lives?
4. **What** can be done to mitigate it?

**💡 Sample opener:**
```
"One alarming environmental issue in my city is air pollution, 
which has been depleting the quality of life for residents — 
a situation that urgently requires sustainable solutions..."
```

💡 **Pro Tip:** Mention **real numbers or examples** (e.g., "Dhaka's AQI reached 300") to boost your Task Response score.
