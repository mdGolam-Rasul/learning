## 🍽️ Topic 1: FOOD

### 🔹 Step 1 — 5 Rich Words + Collocations

| # | Word | Meaning | Collocation / Usage |
|---|------|---------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Palatable** | pleasant to taste | `palatable dish` / `palatable flavor` |
| 2 | **Wholesome** | healthy & nourishing | `wholesome meal` / `wholesome diet` |
| 3 | **Crave** | to strongly desire | `crave sweets` / `crave comfort food` |
| 4 | **Indulge** | to treat oneself | `indulge in dessert` / `indulge occasionally` |
| 5 | **Cuisine** | style of cooking | `local cuisine` / `exotic cuisine` / `traditional cuisine` |

### 🔹 Step 2 — 5 Semantic Sentences

```text
1. Palatable  → The chef prepared a highly palatable dish using fresh herbs.
2. Wholesome  → A wholesome breakfast keeps you energetic throughout the day.
3. Crave      → Many people crave sugary foods when they feel stressed.
4. Indulge    → It's okay to indulge in your favorite dessert occasionally.
5. Cuisine    → Thai cuisine is famous for its bold and spicy flavors.
```

### 🔹 Step 3 — Storytelling (Easy → Big Sentence)

**5 Easy Sentences:**
- Tania loves cooking.
- She tries new recipes.
- Her family enjoys her food.
- She eats healthy meals.
- Sometimes she eats sweets.

**🔥 Big Band 7+ Sentence:**
> *Tania, who loves experimenting with international cuisine, prepares wholesome meals that her family finds incredibly palatable, and although she normally eats healthy, she occasionally indulges in desserts whenever she craves something sweet.*

### 🔹 Step 4 — 7 Sentence Structure Variants

```markdown
1. Simple            → Tania cooks delicious meals.
2. Compound          → Tania loves cuisine, and she cooks every weekend.
3. Complex           → Because Tania enjoys cooking, she tries new recipes daily.
4. Compound-Complex  → Although she eats healthy, Tania indulges sometimes, and she enjoys every bite.
5. Conditional       → If people ate wholesome food, they would feel healthier.
6. Relative Clause   → Tania, who loves cooking, prepares palatable dishes for her family.
7. Participle Phrase → Inspired by global cuisine, Tania started her own food blog.
```

## ⚽ Topic 2: SPORTS

### 🔹 Step 1 — 5 Rich Words + Collocations

| # | Word | Meaning | Collocation / Usage |
|---|------|---------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Perseverance** | continued effort | `show perseverance` / `perseverance pays off` |
| 2 | **Stamina** | physical endurance | `build stamina` / `increase stamina` |
| 3 | **Competitive** | eager to win | `competitive spirit` / `highly competitive` |
| 4 | **Teamwork** | working together | `strong teamwork` / `promote teamwork` |
| 5 | **Exhilarating** | very exciting | `exhilarating game` / `exhilarating experience` |

### 🔹 Step 2 — 5 Semantic Sentences

```text
1. Perseverance → Athletes achieve success through perseverance and dedication.
2. Stamina      → Runners build stamina through regular training.
3. Competitive  → Football is one of the most competitive sports globally.
4. Teamwork     → Strong teamwork is key to winning any match.
5. Exhilarating → Watching the final match was an exhilarating experience.
```

### 🔹 Step 3 — Storytelling (Easy → Big Sentence)

**5 Easy Sentences:**
- Sami plays football.
- He practices daily.
- He never gives up.
- His team works together.
- They won the trophy.

**🔥 Big Band 7+ Sentence:**
> *Sami, who plays football every day, shows remarkable perseverance and builds strong stamina through rigorous training, and thanks to excellent teamwork and a competitive spirit, his team recently won the trophy in an exhilarating final match.*

### 🔹 Step 4 — 7 Sentence Structure Variants

```markdown
1. Simple            → Sami plays football daily.
2. Compound          → Sami has great stamina, and he trains hard.
3. Complex           → Although Sami was tired, he kept practicing.
4. Compound-Complex  → Because teamwork matters, Sami supports his teammates, and they win together.
5. Conditional       → If athletes show perseverance, they will succeed.
6. Relative Clause   → Sami, who loves football, has a strong competitive spirit.
7. Participle Phrase → Exhausted but motivated, Sami continued to train hard.
```

## 📝 Mini Paragraph (Combined)

> *A balanced lifestyle requires both **wholesome** nutrition and regular physical activity. Many people **crave** unhealthy snacks, but those who eat **palatable** yet nutritious **cuisine** tend to feel more energetic. Participating in **competitive** sports builds **stamina**, strengthens **teamwork**, and teaches valuable **perseverance**. Whether you're training for a marathon or simply playing a casual match, the **exhilarating** feeling of achievement — combined with occasional moments to **indulge** in your favorite foods — creates true balance in life.*

📊 **Word count:** ~90 words | Uses all 10 words ✅ | Mixed structures ✅
