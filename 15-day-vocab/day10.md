## 👥 Topic 1: SOCIETY

### 🔹 Step 1 — 5 Rich Words + Collocations

| # | Word | Meaning | Collocation / Usage |
|---|------|---------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Inequality** | unfair difference | `social inequality` / `gender inequality` / `address inequality` |
| 2 | **Marginalized** | pushed to the edge of society | `marginalized groups` / `marginalized communities` |
| 3 | **Cohesion** | unity, togetherness | `social cohesion` / `promote cohesion` |
| 4 | **Stigma** | social shame/disapproval | `social stigma` / `attached stigma` / `remove the stigma` |
| 5 | **Integrate** | to combine / include | `integrate into society` / `integrate minorities` |

### 🔹 Step 2 — 5 Semantic Sentences

```text
1. Inequality    → Social inequality remains one of the biggest challenges of our time.
2. Marginalized  → Governments must protect the rights of marginalized communities.
3. Cohesion      → Strong social cohesion builds peaceful and stable societies.
4. Stigma        → There is still a serious stigma around mental health issues.
5. Integrate     → Immigrants often struggle to integrate into a new culture.
```

### 🔹 Step 3 — Storytelling (Easy → Big Sentence)

**5 Easy Sentences:**
- Rina helps poor people.
- She runs a charity.
- Many people face unfair treatment.
- She gives them support.
- Society is slowly changing.

**🔥 Big Band 7+ Sentence:**
> *Rina, who runs a charity dedicated to helping marginalized families, works tirelessly to reduce social inequality and remove the stigma surrounding poverty, and her efforts to integrate disadvantaged groups into mainstream society have significantly strengthened local cohesion.*

### 🔹 Step 4 — 7 Sentence Structure Variants

```markdown
1. Simple            → Rina helps marginalized people.
2. Compound          → Inequality exists, but Rina fights against it.
3. Complex           → Because social stigma still exists, many people suffer silently.
4. Compound-Complex  → Although challenges remain, Rina keeps working, and society benefits.
5. Conditional       → If we promote cohesion, inequality will gradually decrease.
6. Relative Clause   → Rina, who leads a charity, integrates marginalized families into society.
7. Participle Phrase → Inspired by compassion, Rina dedicated her life to social change.
```

## 💰 Topic 2: MONEY

### 🔹 Step 1 — 5 Rich Words + Collocations

| # | Word | Meaning | Collocation / Usage |
|---|------|---------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Affluent** | wealthy | `affluent family` / `affluent lifestyle` / `affluent society` |
| 2 | **Frugal** | careful with money | `frugal lifestyle` / `frugal habits` / `frugal spender` |
| 3 | **Disposable income** | money left after expenses | `high disposable income` / `limited disposable income` |
| 4 | **Extravagant** | spending too much | `extravagant lifestyle` / `extravagant purchase` |
| 5 | **Financial literacy** | money management knowledge | `improve financial literacy` / `lack financial literacy` |

### 🔹 Step 2 — 5 Semantic Sentences

```text
1. Affluent          → She grew up in an affluent neighborhood in Dhaka.
2. Frugal            → My grandfather lived a frugal lifestyle and saved every taka.
3. Disposable income → Young professionals today have higher disposable income than before.
4. Extravagant       → Celebrities are often criticized for their extravagant spending.
5. Financial literacy → Schools should teach financial literacy from an early age.
```

### 🔹 Step 3 — Storytelling (Easy → Big Sentence)

**5 Easy Sentences:**
- Arman earns a good salary.
- He saves money every month.
- He avoids unnecessary spending.
- He invests wisely.
- He is financially secure.

**🔥 Big Band 7+ Sentence:**
> *Arman, who comes from an affluent family but maintains a frugal lifestyle, carefully manages his disposable income by avoiding extravagant purchases, and thanks to his strong financial literacy, he has built impressive savings and long-term security.*

### 🔹 Step 4 — 7 Sentence Structure Variants

```markdown
1. Simple            → Arman saves money every month.
2. Compound          → Arman earns well, and he invests wisely.
3. Complex           → Although he is affluent, Arman lives a frugal life.
4. Compound-Complex  → Because financial literacy matters, Arman studies investing, and he saves consistently.
5. Conditional       → If people had more financial literacy, they would avoid extravagant debt.
6. Relative Clause   → Arman, who earns a good salary, manages his disposable income wisely.
7. Participle Phrase → Trained in financial planning, Arman built wealth steadily.
```

## 📝 Mini Paragraph (Combined)

> *Modern **society** faces complex challenges related to both wealth distribution and social justice. While **affluent** individuals often enjoy high **disposable income** and occasionally indulge in **extravagant** lifestyles, many **marginalized** communities continue to suffer from **inequality** and persistent social **stigma**. To build stronger **cohesion**, governments should **integrate** underprivileged groups into the economy and promote **financial literacy**, encouraging citizens to adopt more **frugal** and responsible habits — creating a fairer and more sustainable future for all.*

📊 **Word count:** ~85 words | Uses all 10 words ✅ | Mixed structures ✅
