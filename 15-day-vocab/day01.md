## 🧩 The 7 Sentence Structure Variants (Learn Once — Use Forever!)

| # | Structure | Formula | Quick Example |
|---|-----------|---------|---------------|
| 1 | **Simple** | S + V + O | *Students learn English.* |
| 2 | **Compound** | Sentence + *and / but / so* + Sentence | *He studies hard, and he improves.* |
| 3 | **Complex** | Main + *because / although / while* + Dependent | *Although he's tired, he studies.* |
| 4 | **Compound-Complex** | 2 Main + 1 Dependent | *Because he's diligent, he studies daily, and he succeeds.* |
| 5 | **Conditional** | *If / Unless* + clause | *If you study hard, you will succeed.* |
| 6 | **Relative Clause** | Noun + *who / which / that* | *The student who studies hard succeeds.* |
| 7 | **Participle Phrase** | *-ing / -ed* phrase at start | *Determined to succeed, he studies daily.* |

### 🔹 Step 3 — Storytelling (Easy → Big Sentence)

**5 Easy Sentences:**
- Rahul is a student.
- He studies every day.
- He wants to learn English.
- His teacher helps him.
- He is improving fast.

**🔥 Big Band 7+ Sentence:**
> *Rahul, a diligent student determined to acquire advanced English skills, studies every day under the guidance of his teacher, who fosters his confidence and has had a profound impact on his comprehensive growth.*

💡 **Storytelling Tip:** Combining small sentences into one rich sentence demonstrates **fluency, coherence, and grammatical range** — all Band 7+ requirements.

---

### 🔹 Step 4 — 7 Sentence Structure Variants

```markdown
1. Simple            → Rahul studies English daily.
2. Compound          → Rahul studies English daily, and his teacher guides him.
3. Complex           → Because Rahul wants to succeed, he studies every day.
4. Compound-Complex  → Although Rahul struggles at first, he studies daily, and his skills improve rapidly.
5. Conditional       → If Rahul remains diligent, he will acquire fluency soon.
6. Relative Clause   → Rahul, who is a diligent student, acquires new skills quickly.
7. Participle Phrase → Determined to excel, Rahul fosters his skills through daily practice.
```

✅ **Band 7+ Secret:** Same idea, 7 different ways — this is the foundation of grammatical range!

## 🎯 Speaking Challenge

### Cue Card — Education 📚
> *"Describe a teacher who had a big influence on you."*

**Structure your answer:**
1. **Who** was this teacher?
2. **What** did they teach you? *(Use your 5 words!)*
3. **Why** were they special?
4. **How** did they influence your life?

**💡 Sample opener:**
```
"I'd like to describe my English teacher, Mr. Rahman, who had a profound 
impact on my life — a diligent educator who fostered my passion for 
learning and helped me acquire a comprehensive understanding of the language..."
```

💡 **Pro Tip:** For Band 7+ Speaking, always add **one specific memory or story** — examiners reward authenticity.
